Cancel subscription

While we hate to see any Customer go, we understand sometimes our product may no longer be needed.

To cancel your Formlets subscription:

  • Select your Account Name from the top right corner of the Formlet Dashboard
  • Select Payments
  • Select Cancel Subscription
Your account will be returned to a free account
